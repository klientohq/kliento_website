Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

Kliento LLC ("Kliento," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our services, including our website at thekliento.com, our AI Receptionist, Website Chat Widget, and Email Automation Agent (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use our Services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

a) Information You Provide Directly

  • Contact information: name, email address, phone number
  • Business information: company name, industry, service needs
  • Communications: messages you send us via email, web forms, or chat

b) Information Collected Automatically Through Our Services

  • Call data: when you interact with our AI Receptionist, we may collect your phone number, call duration, call recording (where legally permitted and disclosed), and a transcript or summary of the interaction
  • Chat data: messages exchanged with our Website AI Chat Widget, including the content of your queries and our responses
  • Device and usage data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, and timestamps when you visit our website

c) Information Collected on Behalf of Our Clients

Kliento provides AI automation tools to businesses. When our AI Receptionist or other products operate on behalf of a client business, we may process information about that business's customers (including callers, website visitors, or email senders) solely to deliver the service to that client. In these cases, the client business is responsible for its own privacy disclosures to its customers.

2. SMS Communications

Kliento and businesses using our Services may send you SMS (text) messages. These messages fall into two categories:

a) Transactional SMS — Staff Notifications

When you call a business using our AI Receptionist and request that a message be forwarded to a staff member, that message may be delivered via SMS to the relevant staff member's phone number. These are operational notifications triggered directly by your verbal request during the call.

b) Promotional SMS — Booking Links & Offers

During a call with our AI Receptionist, you may be offered the option to receive a text message containing a booking or reservation link, along with a promotional discount (e.g., 10% off an activity booking). SMS messages of this type are sent only if you verbally consent during the call. Your consent is documented as part of the call record.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interactions with the service. Transactional notifications are sent only when triggered by a specific call or interaction. Promotional messages are sent only upon explicit opt-in consent, typically no more than once per interaction.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier messaging and data rates may apply to all SMS messages you receive. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your plan.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any SMS message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further SMS messages will be sent to your number, unless you re-consent.

Help & Support

For assistance with SMS messages, reply HELP to any message you receive from us, or contact us directly at klientohq@gmail.com.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our Services
  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you
  • Send transactional SMS notifications triggered by your interactions
  • Send promotional SMS messages when you have explicitly opted in
  • Fulfill service requests made during AI Receptionist calls (e.g., scheduling, message forwarding, lost & found logging)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our AI systems
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With our clients: If you interacted with an AI system operated by a Kliento client (e.g., a business using our AI Receptionist), we may share the details of that interaction with that client as part of delivering the service.
  • With service providers: We use trusted third-party technology providers (such as Retell AI for voice, Twilio for SMS, and n8n for workflow automation) to operate our Services. These providers process data only on our behalf and under our instructions.
  • For legal compliance: If required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Kliento, our clients, or others.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of Kliento's business, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and comply with our legal obligations. Call records and transcripts are retained for up to 90 days unless a longer retention period is required by a client agreement. You may request deletion of your information by contacting us at klientohq@gmail.com.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications (reply STOP to any message)
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at klientohq@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website or by other appropriate means. Your continued use of our Services after the effective date of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
