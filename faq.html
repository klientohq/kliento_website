Setup & Pricing

Most clients are live within 5–7 business days of signing on. The exact timeline depends on the product and how quickly you can share the information we need (business details, FAQs, calendar access, etc.). We've gotten clients live in as few as 3 days when everything moves quickly.
Pricing is custom to each business — it depends on the product(s) you need, your call volume, and your specific requirements. We don't publish flat rates because a solo contractor has very different needs than a 10-person dental practice. Contact us and we'll give you a clear, honest quote. We price to be accessible — not just for businesses with large budgets.
No long-term lock-in. We work on monthly agreements. If it's not working for you, you can cancel. That said, most clients stick around — because it works.
Ongoing support is included. When your services change, your hours shift, or you want to add a new capability, we update the system. You contact us directly — not a support ticket queue. We also do periodic check-ins to review performance and suggest improvements.

Privacy & Security

Yes. We take data security seriously. Your business information and customer interactions are handled with care — we only collect what's necessary to operate your automation, we don't sell any data, and we use reputable, enterprise-grade infrastructure to power our systems.
Call logs and summaries are generated for every interaction so you can review what happened. Full call recordings can be enabled or disabled based on your preference and your state's recording consent laws. We'll advise you on the right setup for your jurisdiction.
For healthcare clients, we configure systems with HIPAA considerations in mind — limiting what patient information is stored, using appropriate data handling practices, and working with compliant infrastructure where required. Contact us to discuss your specific compliance needs and we'll walk you through what we can and can't do.
