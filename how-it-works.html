The process

From conversation
to live automation.

No lengthy contracts, no months of onboarding. We move fast, build carefully, and get your AI up and running in days.

01
Discovery

We learn your business

We start with a free consultation — a 30–45 minute conversation where we learn how your business operates today. What tasks eat up your time? Where do leads fall through the cracks? What does your team do that an AI could handle instead?

  • Free, no-obligation discovery call
  • We map your current communication workflow
  • Identify the highest-impact automation opportunities
  • Recommend the right products for your specific situation
02
Custom quote

You get a clear proposal

No mystery pricing. After the discovery call, we send you a clear proposal: what we'll build, how it works, what it costs, and what you can expect once it's live. You decide if it's the right fit — zero pressure.

  • Detailed scope of what gets built
  • Transparent pricing, custom to your needs
  • Expected timeline and go-live date
  • No hidden fees, no long-term lock-in
03
Build & configure

We build your system

Once you're on board, we get to work. We configure your AI with your business knowledge — your services, hours, pricing, FAQs, tone of voice, and anything else it needs to represent you accurately. Your input shapes everything.

  • AI trained on your business-specific information
  • Custom voice and tone tuned to match your brand
  • Integrated with your calendar, CRM, or email
  • Built and configured within days, not weeks
04
Testing

We test everything

Before anything goes live, we run the system through real-world scenarios. We test edge cases, unusual questions, and tricky situations so you can be confident your AI handles them the right way — not just the easy ones.

  • Full end-to-end testing before launch
  • You review and approve responses and behavior
  • Adjustments made until it's exactly right
  • Nothing goes live until you sign off
05
Go live

Your AI goes to work

Flip the switch. Your AI is live and handling real interactions — answering calls, chatting with website visitors, or managing your inbox. From day one, it works around the clock so you don't have to.

  • Deployment handled entirely by us
  • Live monitoring during the first 48 hours
  • Quick adjustments if anything needs tuning
  • You're notified of everything happening in real time
06
Ongoing support

We stay in your corner

Your business changes. Your AI should too. We provide ongoing support — updating your system as you add new services, change hours, or need new workflows. You reach us directly. No tickets, no waiting.

  • Direct access to the team that built your system
  • Fast updates as your business evolves
  • Performance check-ins to keep things running smoothly
  • Expand to more products whenever you're ready
