Our story

The phone rings.
Nobody answers.

Before Kliento existed, I was making cold calls. Hundreds of them, every day. What I found was striking: roughly 15 out of every 100 businesses I called actually picked up. The other 85 rang out, went to a full voicemail box, or simply hung up on me with a generic recording.

These weren't distressed companies. They were active, established businesses with real customers, real revenue, and real opportunities slipping through the cracks every single day. The problem wasn't capacity or budget — it was availability. A ringing phone no one answers is a client deciding to call someone else.

That realization is what built Kliento. We build AI systems that make sure businesses are always reachable — answering every call, every email, every website inquiry — so no opportunity goes unaddressed.

"I called hundreds of businesses every day. 85% never answered. That's the problem we solve."

— Camilo Rivas, Founder & CEO, Kliento LLC
